Chennai Rainfall and Reservoir Water Data

In this page, you will see the Rainfall Data and Lake Levels Data in Chennai's Main Reservoirs. The data was collected from https://chennaimetrowater.tn.gov.in/ , the official government website for Chennai Metro Water. The data of Rainfall Level and Lake Level of Five Reservoirs - Poondi, Cholavaram, Red Hills, Chembrambakkam, Veeranam were collected and are shown and visualized in this website.

Source: https://chennaimetrowater.tn.gov.in/
